The Construct typeface emerged as part of the design process for the German Pavilion’s new identity at the Venice Art Biennale.

Reflecting the exhibition’s focus on the care and preservation of buildings, the typeface adopts a modular, architectural character.

The tool enabled the creation of diverse visuals that, despite their variety, are unified by a consistent design language—ensuring a strong and distinctive visual identity.
