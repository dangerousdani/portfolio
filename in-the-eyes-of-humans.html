daniela vogel

In the Eye of Humans demonstrates that human perception is an essential part of artificial intelligence. The interactive installation shows the results of a generative AI, on the one hand as raw data, and on the other hand as visual data. Only in the eyes of people does this data acquire meaning.

Supervision:
Prof. Andreas Ingerl,
Prof. Moritz Schell

exhibited at:
Ars Electronica Festival 2023
